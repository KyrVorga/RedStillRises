Tutorial

How the game works: Red Still Rises is a strategy game where you control a House and its units, aiming to dominate the map by capturing tiles and defeating opposing Houses. Your decisions on movement, resource gathering, and combat will determine your success.

The goal: The objective is to either eliminate all other Houses or hold all their castles for 5 consecutive turns. Use strategy and tactical planning to outmaneuver your opponents and secure victory.

About tiles: Each tile on the map represents a specific biome, which affects the movement cost. When you move your units onto a tile, it becomes your territory, reducing the movement cost for your units on that tile. Controlling more territory gives you a strategic advantage.

How to move:

  • Select a tile: Left-click on a tile to select it.
  • Move units: Right-click on another tile to move your units there.
  • Control options:
    • Hold Ctrl while right-clicking to move one unit at a time. Multiple moves to the same tile are free.
    • Hold Alt while right-clicking to split half your units onto the selected tile.
  • Attacking: Move your units onto an enemy's tile to attack. The stronger force wins, but territory advantages can tip the scales.

What the buttons do:

  • End Turn: Located in the top right, this button ends your turn, allowing the next House to take its actions.
  • Help Menu: Below the end turn button, this button reopens the help screen for guidance.
